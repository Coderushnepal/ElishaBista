Easy Services Nepal
Easy Services Nepal is an android app which provides the platform for the service provider and gives services to the users.

Objectives

1. To find local businesses like Electrician, Painter, Plumber, etc.
2. To create the platform for providing skills one has, through an android application. 3. To provide the services as hourly or daily basis services for the users as per their requirements.
 

Scopes

The major scope of our proposed system is the service sector, i.e. this application will be able to make connections between the service users and service providers. The major field of accomplishment includes facilities like electrician, plumber, painter, carpenter and many more.
