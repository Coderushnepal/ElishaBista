Easy Services Nepal

Introduction

In the past, there was no access to the Internet. All the things are done through physical appearance viz. when there is an issue in an electronic device then people will contact through phone call or they will go at their respective location. The traditional way of selecting a serviceman may lead to distrust and unreliable service. It increases the time complexity and user are unknown about the cost price. It is hard for people to search for serviceman with required talents and qualifications. But now, many software like “FIXIT” is created which are giving the platform for the users not to the service provider. They are not giving the freelancing to the serviceman’s.

Objectives

1. To find local businesses like Electrician, Painter, Plumber, etc.

2. To create the platform for providing skills one has, through an android application.

3. To provide the services as hourly or daily basis services for the users as per their requirements.
